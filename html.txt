<article id="post-941" class="post-941 page type-page status-publish hentry">
	<header class="entry-header">
		<h1 class="entry-title">GrantsDB</h1>	</header><!-- .entry-header -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		<div class="textBox">
<p style="text-align: center"><strong>&gt;&gt; <a href="https://grantsdb.epfl.ch/pap/index.php" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ACCES A GRANTSDB ICI</a> / <a href="https://grantsdb.epfl.ch/pap/index.php" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ACCESS TO GRANTSDB HERE</a> &lt;&lt;</strong></p>
<p><em>(English version below)</em></p>
<p>GrantsDB est une base de données interne à l&#8217;EPFL (<a href="http://linux.epfl.ch/vpn" target="_blank" rel="noopener">connection sécurisée ou en VPN</a>). Les données enregistrées sont confidentielles et les accès restreints aux ayants-droits.</p>
<p>GrantsDB est l&#8217;outil de référence pour :</p>
<ul>
<li>la gestion administrative des <strong>projets de recherche</strong></li>
<li>le suivi des commandes et des paiements pour les <strong>équipements scientifiques </strong>passant par le &#8220;Equipment &amp; Centers Management Office&#8221; (<a href="https://recherche.epfl.ch/eco" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ECO</a>)</li>
<li>la gestion des <strong>contrats industriels </strong>(gérés par le <a class="link_external" href="http://tto.epfl.ch/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">TTO</a>).</li>
</ul>
<p>Conformément à la <a href="/files/recueil_pdf/3.1.1_demande_financement_fr.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">directive Polylex 3.1.1</a>,<strong> toute demande auprès de bailleurs de fonds privés et publics doit être immédiatement enregistrée sur GrantsDB dès sa soumission</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>Pour plus d&#8217;information:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://research-office.epfl.ch/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/Petit-guide-pratique-utilisation-de-GrantsDB.pdf">Petit-guide-pratique-utilisation-de-GrantsDB</a></li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="textBox">
<p>&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;&#8212;-</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;font-style: normal">GrantsDB is an internal EPFL database. The data which are registered in it are confidential and the access to this database is limited to EPFL members in charge of the management of funded research projects only. </span></p>
<p>GrantsDB is the reference tool for:</p>
<ul>
<li>the administrative management of research projects</li>
<li>the orders and payments for scientific equipment via the Equipment &amp; Centers Management Office (<a href="https://recherche.epfl.ch/eco" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ECO</a>)</li>
<li>the management of industrial collaboration agreements (managed by the <a href="http://tto.epfl.ch/hompage_eng" target="_blank" rel="noopener">TTO</a>).</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;font-style: normal">According to the <a href="/files/recueil_pdf/ENG/3.1.1_demande_financement_en.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Directive Polylex 3.1.1</a></span><span style="font-size: 13px;font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;font-style: normal">,</span><strong style="font-size: 13px;font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;font-style: normal"> all submitted applications must be uploaded to GrantsDB on the same day that the proposal is submitted to the funding body</strong><strong style="font-size: 13px;font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;font-style: normal">.</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;font-style: normal">The data contained in the database is confidential </span><span lang="EN-GB"><span lang="EN-GB">(<a href="http://linux.epfl.ch/vpn" target="_blank" rel="noopener">secure connection or with a VPN client</a>)<span style="font-size: 13px;font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;font-style: normal"> and the access is restricted to authorised persons.</span></span></span></p>
</div>
